Visits

01

Boston Birding Festival

Field visit

Three days at the Boston Birding Festival. 140 species recorded, including several rarities. Conversations with amateur and professional ornithologists about the long-term data they are collecting.

The festival is also a ritual — the same people, same paths, same skies, year after year. A living archive of disappearance.

About

Hi! My name is Béatrice Lartigue.
Since 2008, I work as a visual artist and educator — as a co-founder of Lab212 Collective.
I'm currently working on a project exploring the collapse of bird populations called "Whistle Blowers".
In April 2026, I'll have the chance to take part in the Villa Albertine residency research programme in Boston (USA).
This research journal is a record of my various thoughts, research and encounters throughout this project.
